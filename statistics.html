Course Overview

The course "Statistics for Data Science I" is fundamental to understanding and analyzing data effectively. Key topics include probability theory, hypothesis testing, regression analysis, and data visualization. This course forms the foundation for more advanced applications in data science and prepares students to handle real-world data sets with precision and insight.
